About

I'm Omair Jawaid, a Grade 11 student at White Oaks Secondary School in Oakville, Ontario. I'm driven by a genuine interest in engineering, technology, and leadership.

I captain VEX Robotics Team 8433X, compete at the provincial level in DECA as an Ontario Champion (EIB), and co-founded my school's Business Meets Technology Club. I believe the best engineers also think like entrepreneurs.

Outside school I've played rep hockey at the Select and Advanced levels for five years, coach youth soccer and basketball, and stay involved in my community through mentorship and volunteering.
